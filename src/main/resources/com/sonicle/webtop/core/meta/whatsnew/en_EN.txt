		<p>A new module is available to track all operations on mail, calendar, contacts and tasks.<br>

$	5.10.0
	%	Version 5.10.0
	*	<h1>Jitsi Meet integration</h1>
		<p>It is now possible to integrate the Jitsi Meet open source meeting system,
		to have accesso to new features to create and partecipate to online meetings.</p>
		<img src="5.10.0_meeting.png"/>
	*	<h1>Security notifications</h1>
		<p>The system notifies the user with an email anytime that the account
		is accessed through a new device or browser, adding possible gelocalization
		informations, to let the user easily identify unrecognized accesses
		and have a chance to quickly change password.</p>
		<img src="5.10.0_securitynotification.png"/>
	*	<h1>Access control</h1>
		<p>System administrators have a new panel where they can see every
		access to each account, have a quick view of possible repeated accesses
		and verify geolocalization data, enforcing more security control and
		having a chance to take actions in time.</p>
		<img src="5.10.0_adminaccesslog.png"/>
